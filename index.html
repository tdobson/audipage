Dear Aldi UK...

On the 8th of August, my van (number plate: OV08 UBR) stopped at Aldi Knutsford so my girlfriend could buy some reasonably priced painkillers for her knee, whilst the driver took a break from driving.

Somehow, Parking Eye, an Aldi parking contractor, felt the van had overstayed its welcome, and decided they were owed £85 for the 30 minutes overcharge. That's £2.80 per minute!

Giles, Dan, Matthew - This is your store, this happened on your watch - let's take all this frustration, anger, drama and tears and turn it into something positive.

We all know how far £85 will go at Aldi but there are many less fortunate people who can't buy their food from you.

So how about I suggest a more proportionate 'punishment'? If you donate £85's worth of food to the MidCheshire Foodbank - supporting the refugeees, and other vulnerable groups - those who need your food the most, then I will drive to your Knutsford store in our van, and personally take it to the foodbank for you!

Giles, Dan, Matthew can we put this behind us, and do something good for the children who need your food most?

Help the MidCheshire Foodbank today
